Privacy Policy

For the DailyQuote Application

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At DailyQuote, we believe in simplicity and transparency. Our mission is to provide you with daily inspiration without compromising your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines our unwavering commitment to keeping your data safe – by not collecting it in the first place.

DailyQuote does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

What We Don't Collect

To be absolutely clear, here's a list of what DailyQuote does not gather from you or your device:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): This includes your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other data that could identify you personally.
  • Usage Data: We do not track how you use the app, which quotes you view, how long you spend on the app, or any other interaction data.
  • Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, operating system details, or any other information about your device.
  • Sensitive Data: Absolutely no health information, financial details, or any other sensitive categories of data are accessed or stored.
  • Cookies or Tracking Technologies: We do not use cookies, web beacons, or any other tracking technologies within the app.

Why No Data?

The DailyQuote app is designed to be a simple, standalone source of daily wisdom. Its core functionality—displaying a new quote each day—does not require any user data to operate. We believe that a truly focused and private experience is the best experience.

This means you can enjoy your daily dose of inspiration with complete peace of mind, knowing your privacy is fully respected.

Third-Party Services

DailyQuote does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or other services that would collect data from our users. Our commitment to no data collection extends to our lack of reliance on external data-gathering tools.

Children's Privacy

DailyQuote is not directed at children under the age of 13. Given that we do not collect any personal data from any users, the privacy of children is inherently protected by our no-data policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
